Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long does a garage floor installation take?

Most standard garage floor projects are completed within one to two days, depending on the floor condition and coating system.

When can I walk or park on the new floor?

Light foot traffic is usually possible after 24 hours. Vehicles should typically wait 48 to 72 hours.

Are garage floor coatings durable?

Yes. Professional coatings are designed to resist stains, chemicals, impacts, heavy loads and temperature changes.

Can damaged or cracked concrete be coated?

In many cases, cracks and surface damage can be repaired before the coating is applied.
